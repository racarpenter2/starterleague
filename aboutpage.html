I recently moved to Chicago to pursue a startup called Tribunat Valuation with two business partners. Now, I'm proud to say I'm a student at The Starter League!!!

Tribunat Valuation LLC (the Company or Tribunat) provides a new economic model for equity analysis and valuation information for investors to make wise investment decisions. The Tribunat Concept is a knowledge-based community encompassing three functions: Valuation, Exchange and Shares.


The next evolution in social media and knowledge management is the formation of knowledge communities, mixing the creation of specialized insight of value and social collaboration with an obligation to advance the competency of the community as a whole.


The Tribunat community progresses the intrinsic value approach to making investment decisions. With each function of the Tribunat Concept, the knowledge community has the information, insight and credibility to conspire in determining the intrinsic value of U.S. publicly traded companies. This provides the community of experienced and interested investors the intelligence they need to make wise investment decisions.
